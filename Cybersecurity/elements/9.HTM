Zomatoooo! IDOR in Saved Payments

Title: Zomatoooo! 保存支付中的 IDOR

Author: Prateek Srivastava

Link: https://prateeksrivastavaa.medium.com/zomatoooo-idor-in-saved-payments-f8c014879741

Source: medium

It’s been a while since I last posted about my findings. This time it’s the food giant Zomato. I had a sudden food craving and headed to my go-to-stop, Zomato. I browsed through the restaurants and dishes when I found something appetizing. I quickly added it to the cart and just as I was about to proceed with payment, I noticed that they presented me with some of my previously used payment methods. It scratched that part of my mind, and yes! It was time to do some intercepting. Burp Suite time guys!

自从我上次发布我的发现已经有一段时间了。这次是食品巨头 Zomato。我突然有了食物 cravings，便前往我常去的地方 Zomato。我浏览了餐厅和菜品，发现了一些令人垂涎的东西。我迅速将其添加到购物车中，就在我准备进行支付时，我注意到他们向我展示了一些我之前使用过的支付方式。这激发了我脑海中的某个部分，是的！是时候进行一些拦截了。Burp Suite 时间，伙计们！

I fired up my Burp Suite and started intercepting all the requests going through my phone. As I moved ahead to make the payment once again, I found a request sending a lot of parameters to fetch default_payment_options. One of those parameters was payment_method_id which seemed very interesting. Hmmm, what if I increment or decrement the payment_method_id by 1 or 2 to see if I can fetch someone else’s payment method details.

我启动了我的 Burp Suite，并开始拦截我手机上所有的请求。当我再次进行支付时，我发现有一个请求发送了很多参数以获取 default_payment_options。其中一个参数是 payment_method_id，这看起来非常有趣。嗯，如果我将 payment_method_id 增加或减少 1 或 2，看看是否能获取到其他人的支付方式详情，会怎么样呢。

I changed the payment_method_id to a random number, and to my surprise, I could see the payment information of some random dude. What in this freaking world? Lol. I was able to see the cardholder’s name, the first 6 and last 4 digits of the card, the bank’s name, and more.

我把 payment_method_id 改成了一个随机数字，令我惊讶的是，我竟然看到了某个随机家伙的支付信息。这到底是什么鬼？哈哈。我能够看到持卡人的名字、卡的前 6 位和后 4 位数字、银行的名称等等。

Now figuring out that the payment_method_id’s were sequential wasn’t a hard task, so it was time to do some automation. Bruteforcing Time! Hoping for no rate-limiting in place, I fired up my intruder and started to bruteforce the last 4 digits of the payment_method_id to see if I could fetch details of random people without even getting rate-limited.

现在发现 payment_method_id 是连续的并不是一项困难的任务，所以是时候进行一些自动化了。开始暴力破解时间！希望没有速率限制，我启动了我的入侵者，开始暴力破解 payment_method_id 的最后四位数字，看看我是否可以在没有被速率限制的情况下获取随机人的详细信息。

It didn’t disappoint me, there was no rate-limiting and I could send as many requests as I wanted. Essentially, there was no proper check at the backend, leading to an IDOR vulnerability. I went to HackerOne, drafted a comprehensive report with a PoC video, and reported it to Zomato. They, for real, surprised me by taking just 43 minutes to reward a bounty and push a patch for the same. Godspeed guys!

这没有让我失望，没有速率限制，我可以发送任意数量的请求。基本上，后端没有进行适当的检查，导致了 IDOR 漏洞。我去了 HackerOne，撰写了一份详细的报告和一个 PoC 视频，并向 Zomato 报告了这个问题。他们真的让我惊讶，仅用了 43 分钟就奖励了赏金并推送了补丁。祝你们好运！
